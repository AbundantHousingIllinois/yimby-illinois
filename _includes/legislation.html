Legislation We Support

We work with legislators on both sides of the aisle to advance practical, bipartisan housing reforms in Springfield.

Active

IL Missing Middle Housing Act

Would legalize 2-4 unit residential buildings statewide, creating thousands of new homes near jobs and transit.

Passed

People Over Parking Act

Eliminated parking minimums near transit stations, reducing construction costs and encouraging walkable development.

Active

ADU Legalization Act

Allows accessory dwelling units on all residential lots in Illinois, giving homeowners new options for housing family and generating income.
