Who We Are

Building a Movement for Housing Abundance

YIMBY Illinois is a statewide organization advocating for common-sense housing reforms that make our communities more affordable, more inclusive, and more vibrant. We believe that everyone deserves a place to call home in the neighborhoods and cities they love.

For decades, restrictive zoning laws and burdensome regulations have limited housing construction across Illinois. The result is a growing affordability crisis that affects renters and homeowners alike, from Chicago to Champaign, from Rockford to Carbondale. Working families are squeezed by rising costs, young people are priced out of the communities where they grew up, and seniors struggle to find affordable options to age in place.

Affiliated with Abundant Housing Illinois and a proud member of the IL Homes for All coalition, YIMBY Illinois brings together neighbors, advocates, policymakers, and housing professionals to push for state-level policies that unlock housing production. We know that when we build more homes of all types, everyone benefits.
